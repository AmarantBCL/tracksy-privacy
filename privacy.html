Privacy Policy for Tracksy

Last updated: August 29, 2025

1. Data Collection

Tracksy does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All information (such as expenses, incomes, and savings goals) is stored locally on your device. The app does not require creating an account or providing personal details.

2. Data Usage

Your financial data is used only within the app to provide tracking and visualization features. We do not share your data with third parties.

3. Permissions

Tracksy does not request any sensitive permissions (camera, contacts, location, etc.).

4. Security

Since all data remains on your device, you are responsible for maintaining your device’s security (such as using a password, PIN, or biometric lock).

5. Children’s Privacy

Tracksy is designed for general use and does not target children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if necessary. Updates will be reflected in this document.
