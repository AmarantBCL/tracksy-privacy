Privacy Policy for Tracksy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

1. Data Collection

Tracksy does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All information (such as expenses, incomes, savings goals, budgets, and profile data) is stored locally on your device. The app does not require creating an account or providing personal details.

2. Data Usage

Your financial data is used only within the app to provide tracking and visualization features. We do not share your data with third parties.

3. Permissions

Tracksy does not request any sensitive permissions (camera, contacts, location, etc.).

4. Security

Since all data remains on your device, you are responsible for maintaining your device’s security (such as using a password, PIN, or biometric lock). Additionally, the app features an optional, built-in security functionality that allows you to set up a custom PIN for enhanced data protection. Using this feature is completely optional.

5. Children’s Privacy

Tracksy is designed for general use and does not target children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if necessary. Updates will be reflected in this document.
